Volatility Transmission in Commodity Markets

Evidence from Energy, Mining, and Agriculture Using GARCH Models

Research Topic

This research investigates volatility transmission mechanisms across commodity markets, focusing on the sectors of energy, mining, and agriculture. The study employs GARCH models to quantify and analyze volatility spillovers, providing insights for risk management in an increasingly interconnected global economy.

Research Objectives

  • • Explore Sector-Specific Spillover Channels
  • • Investigate Volatility Magnitude Changes
  • • Analyze Macroeconomic Impacts

Methodology Highlights

  • • GARCH Models Implementation
  • • Dynamic Conditional Correlation Analysis
  • • Cross-Sector Volatility Assessment
